2_2 概率的基本概念

本书假定所有的连续随机变量都拥有概率密度函数（probability density functions，PDF)

普通密度函数都是具有均值μ\muμ和方差σ2{\sigma}^{2}σ2的一维正态分布（normal distribution）。正态分布的概率密度函数可用高斯函数表示：

p(x)=(2πσ2)−12exp⁡{−12(x−μ)2σ2}p(x)=\left(2 \pi \sigma^{2}\right)^{-\frac{1}{2}} \exp \left\{-\frac{1}{2} \frac{(x-\mu)^{2}}{\sigma^{2}}\right\} p(x)=(2πσ2)−21​exp{−21​σ2(x−μ)2​}

缩写为N(x;μ,σ2){N}\left(x ; \mu, \sigma^{2}\right)N(x;μ,σ2)

2_4_2 实例

测量数据z：摄像机

控制数据u：机器手

门的状态x：开（open）闭（closed）

初始状态x_0：未知

先验概率：

bel(X0=open)=0.5bel(X0=closed)=0.5bel(X_0=open)=0.5\\ bel(X_0=closed)=0.5\\ bel(X0​=open)=0.5bel(X0​=closed)=0.5

假定机器人传感器有噪声。噪声特性通过下面的条件概率表征：

p(Zt=sense_open∣Xt=is_open)=0.6p(Zt=sense_close∣Xt=is_open)=0.4p(Zt=sense_open∣Xt=is_close)=0.2p(Zt=sense_close∣Xt=is_close)=0.8p(Z_t=sense\_open|X_t=is\_open)=0.6\\ p(Z_t=sense\_close|X_t=is\_open)=0.4\\ p(Z_t=sense\_open|X_t=is\_close)=0.2\\ p(Z_t=sense\_close|X_t=is\_close)=0.8\\ p(Zt​=sense_open∣Xt​=is_open)=0.6p(Zt​=sense_close∣Xt​=is_open)=0.4p(Zt​=sense_open∣Xt​=is_close)=0.2p(Zt​=sense_close∣Xt​=is_close)=0.8

假设机器人使用操作器把门拉开。

如果门已经开了，将保持开着；如果门关着，有0_8的概率将门打开。

p(Xt=is_open∣Ut=push,Xt−1=is_open)=1p(Xt=is_closed∣Ut=push,Xt−1=is_open)=0p(Xt=is_open∣Ut=push,Xt−1=is_closed)=0.8p(Xt=is_closed∣Ut=push,Xt−1=is_closed)=0.2\begin{array}{l} p(X_t=is\_open|U_t=push,X_{t-1}=is\_open)=1\\ p(X_t=is\_closed|U_t=push,X_{t-1}=is\_open)=0\\ p(X_t=is\_open|U_t=push,X_{t-1}=is\_closed)=0.8\\ p(X_t=is\_closed|U_t=push,X_{t-1}=is\_closed)=0.2\\ \end{array} p(Xt​=is_open∣Ut​=push,Xt−1​=is_open)=1p(Xt​=is_closed∣Ut​=push,Xt−1​=is_open)=0p(Xt​=is_open∣Ut​=push,Xt−1​=is_closed)=0.8p(Xt​=is_closed∣Ut​=push,Xt−1​=is_closed)=0.2​

也可以选择不使用操作器，这种情况下，世界状态不会改变。

p(Xt=is_open∣Ut=do_nothing,Xt−1=is_open)=1p(Xt=is_closed∣Ut=do_nothing,Xt−1=is_open)=0p(Xt=is_open∣Ut=do_nothing,Xt−1=is_closed)=0p(Xt=is_closed∣Ut=do_nothing,Xt−1=is_closed)=1\begin{array}{l}p(X_t=is\_open|U_t=do\_nothing,X_{t-1}=is\_open)=1\\ p(X_t=is\_closed|U_t=do\_nothing,X_{t-1}=is\_open)=0\\ p(X_t=is\_open|U_t=do\_nothing,X_{t-1}=is\_closed)=0\\ p(X_t=is\_closed|U_t=do\_nothing,X_{t-1}=is\_closed)=1\\ \end{array} p(Xt​=is_open∣Ut​=do_nothing,Xt−1​=is_open)=1p(Xt​=is_closed∣Ut​=do_nothing,Xt−1​=is_open)=0p(Xt​=is_open∣Ut​=do_nothing,Xt−1​=is_closed)=0p(Xt​=is_closed∣Ut​=do_nothing,Xt−1​=is_closed)=1​

假定在时刻t=1时，机器人没有采取任何控制动作，但它检测到门是开的（检测会有噪声，不确定性）。结果（门的状态）的置信度可以由贝叶斯滤波计算。输入分别是：之前置信度bel(x_0)、控制u_1=do_nothing、测量“sense_open”。由于状态空间有限（只有开和闭两种），所以算法中的积分结果是有限和：

bel‾(x1)=∫p(x1∣u1,x0)bel(x0)dx0=p(x1∣U1=do_nothing,X0=is_open)bel(X0=is_open)+p(x1∣U1=do_nothing,X0=is_closed)bel(X0=is_closed)\begin{aligned} \overline{bel}(x_{1})&=\int p(x_{1}\mid u_{1},x_{0})bel(x_{0})dx_{0} \\ &=p(x_{1}\mid U_{1}=\mathrm{do\_nothing},X_{0}=\mathrm{is\_open})bel(X_{0}=\mathrm{is\_open}) + \\ &p(x_1\mid U_1=\mathrm{do\_nothing},X_0=\mathrm{is\_closed})bel(X_0=\mathrm{is\_closed}) \end{aligned} bel(x1​)​=∫p(x1​∣u1​,x0​)bel(x0​)dx0​=p(x1​∣U1​=do_nothing,X0​=is_open)bel(X0​=is_open)+p(x1​∣U1​=do_nothing,X0​=is_closed)bel(X0​=is_closed)​

分别计算X_1的两种情况：

bel‾(X1=is_open)=p(X1=is.open∣U1=do.nothing.X0=is.open)bel(X0=is.open)+p(X1=is.open∣U1=do.nothing.X0=is.closed)bel(X0=is.closed)=1×0.5+0×0.5=0.5\begin{aligned}&\overline{bel}(X_1=\text{is\_open})\\ &=p(X_1=\text{is.open}\mid U_1=\text{do.nothing.}X_0=\text{is.open})bel(X_0=\text{is.open})\\&\quad+p(X_1=\text{is.open}\mid U_1=\text{do.nothing.}X_0=\text{is.closed})bel(X_0=\text{is.closed})\\ &=1\times0.5+0\times 0.5=0.5\end{aligned} ​bel(X1​=is_open)=p(X1​=is.open∣U1​=do.nothing.X0​=is.open)bel(X0​=is.open)+p(X1​=is.open∣U1​=do.nothing.X0​=is.closed)bel(X0​=is.closed)=1×0.5+0×0.5=0.5​

bel‾(X1=is_closed)=p(X1=is_open∣U1=do_nothing,X0=is_open)bel(X0=is_open)+p(X1=is_open∣U1=do_nothing,X0=is_closed)bel(X0=is_closed)=1×0_5+0×0_5=0_5\begin{aligned}&\overline{bel}(X_1=\text{is\_closed})\\ &=p(X_1=\text{is\_open}\mid U_1=\text{do\_nothing},X_0=\text{is\_open})bel(X_0=\text{is\_open})\\&\quad+p(X_1=\text{is\_open}\mid U_1=\text{do\_nothing},X_0=\text{is\_closed})bel(X_0=\text{is\_closed})\\ &=1\times0\_5+0\times 0\_5=0\_5\end{aligned} ​bel(X1​=is_closed)=p(X1​=is_open∣U1​=do_nothing,X0​=is_open)bel(X0​=is_open)+p(X1​=is_open∣U1​=do_nothing,X0​=is_closed)bel(X0​=is_closed)=1×0_5+0×0_5=0_5​
